What is Dakgangjeong?

Dakgangjeong is crispy Korean fried chicken pieces glazed in a sticky, sweet, and spicy sauce. The dakgangjeong recipe uses bite sized boneless chicken pieces, so it cooks up very fast for a quick snack! The easiest Korean fried chicken recipe you can make!
